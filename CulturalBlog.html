What is Emotional Intelligence?

For me emotional intelligence means having the ability to understand, use, and manage your own emotions in positive ways to relieve stress, communicate effectively, empathize with others, overcome challenges and defuse conflict. This is highly effective when you're in a management role when you're dealing with different types of personalities, cultures and different views. An important factor is that for the majority of people it depends on your environment when you grew up, this factor has a great effect on emotional intelligence. If the child is nurtured in an environment where it is taught and sees emotional awareness, then this will increasingly encourage a higher EQ.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ, is a number derived from a standardized intelligence test, Emotional intelligence refers to a person's ability to perceive, control, evaluate, and express emotions.

Why is it important?

A high EQ helps individuals to communicate better, reduce their anxiety and stress, defuse conflicts, improve relationships, empathize with others, and effectively overcome life's challenges. Our emotional intelligence affects the quality of our lives because it influences our behavior and relationships.
