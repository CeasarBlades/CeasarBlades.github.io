Different Methods to Problem Solving

Pseudocode

My understanding of pseudocode is basically a written recipe or steps about constructing steps to solve a problem. The classic example i could use to explain this my non-technical mate, would be think of pseudocode as a recipe to bake a pizza. Example 1. get some flour, 2. get salt, 3. get the yeast and so on... Until you've complete the recipe or task, so it's basically recipe to your work. Pretty much like a comment.

Trying something

Trying something is my second most used form of problem solving after coming up with a plan.

Rubber ducky method

In aviation we use a similar method called Feynman technique, instead of talking to a duck, its always good to explain to yourself or try explaining what your trying to solve to a 5 year old. When explaing the problem or concept to a 5 year old in simple terms. This not only helps with your understanding it also helps with you breaking the problem into smaller steps.
